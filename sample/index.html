Possible Health Conditions

  • Cold

    The common cold is a viral infection that affects the nose and throat. Symptoms may include cough, sore throat, runny or stuffy nose, and fever.

  • Flu

    The flu is a viral infection that can cause fever, cough, body aches, and other symptoms. It is highly contagious and can be serious in some cases.

  • Allergies

    Allergies occur when the immune system overreacts to a substance that is normally harmless. Symptoms may include sneezing, itching, runny nose, and watery eyes.
